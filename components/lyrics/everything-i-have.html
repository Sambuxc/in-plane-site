Everything I Have

Peter Taylor

Verse 1

Sometimes I like to think about yesterday
even though I know it's filled with pain and sorrow
my life has been like living in a raging storm
not knowing if I will meet the dawn

Verse 2

My path has taken me in many directions
many doors have closed right in my face
many times I´ve felt like running leave the dream behind
lay on my bed going out of my mind

Pre-Chorus

But then one morning everything changed
suddenly I saw everything I have

Chorus

Like the air I breathe
the grass growing under my feet
a face to smile at people passing by
and up above that great big wonderful sky

Verse 3

To now feel this way is really quite amazing
and my life has changed for the better
no longer do I stand in the raging storm
I can't wait for a new day to dawn

Pre-Chorus

Because of that morning when everything changed
suddenly I saw everything I have

Outro

Like the air I breathe
the grass growing under my feet
a face to smile at people passing by
and up above that great big wonderful sky

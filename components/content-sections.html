Backstory

It’s time to give you the heads-up on what you might be getting yourself into. This is a short lead-in about the project overview and the narrative tone for the homepage layout.

Yes, I am Stuart K. Taylor, and if you ask me nicely, I might one day tell you what the K stands for. I recently decided to take my love of writing into the world of cyberspace; yes, yes I know I am a banana head, anyway enough of the humour now, so let’s get this thing done.

To tell you the truth, I'm not here chasing big numbers, far from it. In fact, if you don’t like the content, it’s no bother, I won’t lose any sleep hoping you'll join the party or how many followers I’ve got, I love what I do and that’s more than enough.

01 Nobody's Child

We meet central character Maurice as he is driving down the M3 from London in his campervan during a nasty summer thunderstorm on his way to the south coastal town of Bournemouth. He sings and plays guitar in the street for a living, but due to the Londoners giving him a wide berth, he believes working the south coast in the summer is a good plan.

As the thunderstorm seems relentless, he decides to pull into the next service station and give himself a serious talking to. Sitting at a table in the café area, he fumbles around in his backpack for a pen to make some notes on his finances when an elderly lady appears and offers him one.

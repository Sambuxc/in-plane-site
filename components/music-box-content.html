03 Music Box

Welcome to the Music Box, a place where you can take the weight off, chill if you fancy, and spend no more than around a quarter of an hour listening to three songs I wrote along the way.

On the subject of listening to the songs I'd like to add that I've included the lyrics of each song as the words and music were written as one and should be experienced in the same way.

I started writing songs many moons ago, and I've never stopped loving the process of writing a song and lyrics that fit, offering up the possibility that our Music Box will be with us for a good while.

02 Short Stories

Welcome to Short Stories, a collection of a diverse collection of work that can be read in round about an hour.

What inspired me to write them is that if you travel on a train or some other way of getting to work you can counter the boredom of the journey with a short read.

Initially I have given you a choice of three that include a summary of the content and have another ten in the final proof reading stage that will keep you topped up.

The purpose of inplanesite.me is to diversify as much as is possible with words and music, and in the process, create a compendium of choices that sidle smoothly into your day.
